# Quest Encouragement Design Notes

## v0.6.18

Quest encouragement is an influence mechanic, not direct control.

## Current Placeholder

```text
Cost: 35g
Duration: 1 in-game day
Effect: adventurers pursue Slime quest targets with more urgency
```

## Current Effects

```text
movement speed toward quest targets increased
combat cooldown reduced
Quest Notice floating text
quest status shows encouragement active
```

## Why Cost Matters

The player should not press Encourage every time.

It should be used when:
- a threat is growing too fast
- adventurers are not naturally addressing the issue
- the player accepts the budget cost
- the reward/risk tradeoff makes sense

## Future Cost Inputs

Later, encouragement may cost:
- gold
- paper/notices
- messenger labor
- Guild Hall staff time
- reputation
- influence points
- quest reward deposits

## Future Assignment Options

Possible future versions:
- general encouragement
- class-specific notice
- favorite adventurer invitation
- resident assignment
- paid bounty increase
- emergency notice during monster growth
