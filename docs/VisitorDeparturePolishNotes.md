# Visitor Departure Polish Notes

## Current Placeholder

Visitors currently disappear when they leave the local region.

That is acceptable during prototype testing because it proves the pool cycling works.

## Future Polish Requirement

Visitors should eventually walk back toward the village entrance before disappearing.

Suggested flow:
1. Visitor decides to leave.
2. Visitor pathfinds to village entrance.
3. Floating text shows departure reason.
4. Visitor exits at entrance/edge.
5. Visitor returns to available pool.

## Why It Matters

Instant disappearance is useful for testing but visually rough.

Walking out will make the town feel more believable and reduce player confusion.
