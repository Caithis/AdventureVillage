# Future Fog of War and World Map Scope Notes

## Long-Term World Map Direction

The world map should eventually become a strategic regional-management layer.

## Future Systems

```text
zones
fog of war
known monsters
unknown monsters
known nests
nest growth
nest reduction
known dungeons
active dungeons/portals
danger pressure
scouting reports
guild notices
```

## Intended Player Role

The player should manage the regional state of affairs through the guild.

The player should not directly puppet adventurers, but should influence priorities through:

```text
quests
notices
rewards
encouragement costs
contracts
resident assignments later
```

## Important Constraint

Information should matter.

The player should not know every threat at the start.

Good quest options should depend on what the guild has discovered.
